About


An idea. A solution.

At Convenire, we only have one mission, and that is to help people around the world gather and enjoy the little things in life. Tired of spending time and energy organizing events? Let us help your guests help you.

With our solution, everyone participates and things get done. Fast. With the click of a button, you give us some initial information (max. 5 mins.) and the rest is done. Have your guests vote for the time and place with the options you have provided and let them sign up for tasks you wish to be done before the grand event. It's that simple.

Convenire was an idea brought to life by Ricardo, Andre and Philippe, one Sunday evening as they were casually enjoying a glass of Hennessy by some fireplace. "Hey, wouldn't it be neat if we could arrange a meeting on some website without having to sign up? Oh God." and they never looked back. 20 000 hours of development later, Convenire was no longer just a thought.
